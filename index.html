About Me

From a mechanical engineering background to a successful highspeed trader, data analysis and manipulation have always been at the focal point of my interests. The ability to swiftly observe samples from both a macro and micro level allows me to place both scopes together to build a unique solution and project. Moreover, the ability to teach and give back to my community and friends is my long-term goal of leaving an impact on the world.

These passions gave me the confidence to strive to become a lifelong learner and tackle both web development, software development, and algorithms. I became fascinated with both displaying data on the screen and trying to automate and speed up tasks at a large scale. The realization of a myriad of teaching and learning styles boosts my desire to accomplish these goals I have set.
